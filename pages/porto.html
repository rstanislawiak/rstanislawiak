Report on the Inaugural Student Assembly in Strasbourg

Radoslaw Stanislawiak

April 10, 2022

The international conference was held from 6th to 10th of April 2022 in Porto, Portugal by Erasmus Student Network. Association brought together students from European universities to discuss the future of mobility and the new Erasmus+ program 2021-2027. Main points was the student exchange development to learn more about the new programs and mobilities. The focus was on promoting exchange programs and different educational institutions from all over Europe.
